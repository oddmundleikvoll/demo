About Superhuman

Superhuman is a wholesale exporter of high-quality health supplements at fair prices. We work closely with Nordic manufacturers to curate reliable, store-ready products that are clear on labeling and easy to stock.

What we do

We source and distribute only the best supplements and herbal products from trusted suppliers. Our focus is on consistent quality, transparent documentation, and predictable logistics for retailers and distributors.

Made in the North

Many of the products we carry are crafted in the North, among clean air, rugged coastlines, and wide-open landscapes. The setting inspires careful raw-material selection and a calm, methodical approach to production.

Wholesale & export

As a wholesaler and exporter, we support private labels and multi-market assortments. Need assortments, planograms, or batch documentation? We can help you build the right mix for your shelves.
